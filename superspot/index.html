A pet that can follow commands like a dog (such as go, stop, heel) and play fetch by following a ball that will be thrown by a user, then bringing it back to the user. Commands are given through a phone, and the fetch capability is enabled with ultrasound.

Design

  • Software/Firmware

    Ultrasound and voice commands processing.

  • Electrical

    Sound processing, voltage conversion.

  • Mechanical

    Robot body, ability to pick up a ball/toy.
