Pie Puzzle

Our objective was to build an puzzle box with mechanical, electrical, and software components. We wanted to make something fun and engaging that our peers would be excited to try. The final product is a pie-themed puzzle box (cylinder?) with two puzzles for the user to solve. If both puzzles are solved, they will find the key to their prize.
